Book of Commandments Chapter 16 (1833)

Compared with Doctrine and Covenants Section 19 (2013)

The phrases in verse 1 are re-arranged. This was likely done to fix the awkward beginninging of the text, since the chapter heading already provides somewhat of an introduction, which language has since been discontinued.

Verses 13-15 are expanded significantly to clarify the source and nature of the call to repentence.

Extra clarification given in verse 18 to clear up potential confusion.

Verse 20 is changed in a similar way as verses 13-15, but to a lesser extend. The phrase of being commanded "by" God's Almighty power is again rephrased to being commanded to repent.

The concepts in verses 21-24 are broken up and revised for clarity.

Stylistic change in verses 25, 26, and 28 to make the language more consistent.

Update in verse 32 to reflect future perspectives.

Verses 35 and 36 expanded for clarity.
