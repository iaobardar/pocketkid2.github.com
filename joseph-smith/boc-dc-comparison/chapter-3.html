Book of Commandments Chapter 3 (1833)

Compared with Doctrine and Covenants Section 4 (2013)

Change 1 (verse 6): The list printed in the Book of Commandments with an &c (etc) is expanded upon. It is unclear exactly why the full list was not recorded in the original manuscripts or what methodology was used in expanding upon it.
