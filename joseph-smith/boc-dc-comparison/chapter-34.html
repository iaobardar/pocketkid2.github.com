A Revelation to Thomas, given in Fayette, New-York, September, 1830.

Revelation given through Joseph Smith the Prophet to Thomas B. Marsh, September 1830. The occasion was immediately following a conference of the Church. Thomas B. Marsh had been baptized earlier in the month and had been ordained an elder in the Church before this revelation was given.
