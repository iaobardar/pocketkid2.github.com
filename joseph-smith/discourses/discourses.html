All Known Joseph Smith Discourses

An attempt to pool together all the known discourse reports of Joseph Smith, both handwritten and printed, and compared side-by-side.

Transcripts and sources primarily from JosephSmithPapers.org. Brackets are used for other assumed sources that are not viewable on JosephSmithPapers.org

Minutes are not included unless they represent or reconstruct a discourse that was captured on JosephSmithPapers.org. Simply discourse summaries are not included.
