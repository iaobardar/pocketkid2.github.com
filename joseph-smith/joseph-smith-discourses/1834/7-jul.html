Discourse of Joseph Smith given around July 7th, 1834

2 Manuscript sources, 2 Printed editions

Event: Council of High Priests

Location: Lyman Wight's house, Clay County, Missouri

This is a brief sketch of a discourse of Joseph Smith, given in Missouri, at the organization of the Missouri High Council, which was considered the presidency of the church in Missouri, in Joseph Smith's abscence. Frederick G. Williams was the official clerk, and the minutes were copied into Minute Book 2 (Far West Record) in 1838 by Levi Richards and Ebenezer Robinson. It was edited lightly and copied into the Manuscript History in August 1843 by Willard Richards, and printed in February 1846 in the Times and Seasons "History of Joseph Smith" serial as well as the later B. H. Roberts History of the Church.
