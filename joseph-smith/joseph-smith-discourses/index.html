All Known Joseph Smith Discourses

An attempt to pool together all the known discourse reports of Joseph Smith, both handwritten and printed, and compared side-by-side.

For Stage 1, only true discourse reports will be included, not summaries and mentions of discourses. Transcripts and versions taken from Joseph Smith Papers unless otherwise noted by brackets.
