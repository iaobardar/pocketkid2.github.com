Joseph Smith Discourses

An attempt to pool together the known discourse reports of Joseph Smith, both handwritten and printed, and compared side-by-side.

This is a work in progress, and will begin by focusing on capture discourse reports that contain actual dialogue, not just summaries, although even the best reports are not word for word. Transcripts and versions taken from Joseph Smith Papers unless otherwise noted. N/A for the present time means Not Accessible. For documents that may or may not be considered a discourse, R stands for "remarks", I for "instruction", M for "meeting" (as classified by the Joseph Smith Papers website calendar).
