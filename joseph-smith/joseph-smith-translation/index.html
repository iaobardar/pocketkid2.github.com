Joseph Smith Translation vs King James Version

A side-by-side comparison of the King James Bible with various manuscript and pritned editions of the "New Translation" or "Inspired Version", also known as the Joseph Smith Translation (JST)

1. Chapters with no link contain no changes, and are either marked as correct in the manuscript or are not listed in said manuscript.

2. Chapters with regular links contain changes in the manuscript, but are not written out in full, requiring the marked Bible.

3. Chapters with bold links contain the entire text of the chapter written out, thus not requiring the marked Bible to determine insertion and change points.

4. Chapters with bold and italic links contain text published in the Pearl of Great Price. (Moses and Joseph Smith-Matthew)

Credits: All manuscript transcripts and historical details are taken from josephsmithpapers.org. The Moses references corresponding to Genesis are taken from "The Complete Joseph Smith Translation of the Old Testament" by Thomas A. Wayment (Deseret Book, 2009).
