"History/Life of Joseph Smith"

Index of transcripts for the newspaper serial

This page exists to fill the gap until a verified transcript is available from the Joseph Smith Papers website.

The original printing of the History of Joseph Smith was in the Times and Seasons in Nauvoo, Illinois, from March 1842 to February 1846, and in the Deseret News in Salt Lake City, Utah, from November 1851 to January 1858. It was reprinted in the Millennial Star in England from June 1842 to May 1863.

The tables below attempt to show the various installments and dates of the serial for both the original and reprint. No attempt has been made to match dates and newspaper issues with page numbers from History of the Church although that would be a useful feature to have in the future.

The Joseph Smith papers website reports that the Times and Seasons issues were edited by Joseph Smith and John Taylor, and the Deseret News issues were edited by Willard Richards, Albert Carrington, and Elias Smith.
